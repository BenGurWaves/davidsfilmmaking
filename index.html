Why Do I Make Films?

I challenged myself to create something I’d be proud of for the rest of my life. This film is that. Even though there’s no groundbreaking cinematography or lighting, it dives deep into my filmmaking struggles and my life as an artist.

Please watch this in one sitting — taking breaks will ruin the flow. This is by far the most tasking and hardest thing I’ve ever done. I really enjoyed making it, and I hope you enjoy it too.
