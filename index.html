Why Do I Make Films?

I challenged myself to create something that I was going to be proud of for the rest of my life. This film is that, even though there is no groundbreaking cinematography or lighting. It dives deep into my filmmaking struggles and my life as an artist.

Please watch this in one sitting — taking breaks will ruin the film. This is by far the most tasking and hardest thing I have ever done. I really enjoyed making this, and I hope you enjoy it as well.
